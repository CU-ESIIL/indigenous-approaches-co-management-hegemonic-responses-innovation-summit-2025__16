# Data Access

Use this page to keep track of datasets supporting the Indigenous Approaches to Co-Management sprint. Update links and notes as you finalize sources.

## Spatial layers
- **PAD-US 3.0** — Download from the [USGS GAP portal](https://www.usgs.gov/programs/gap-analysis-project/science/protected-areas). Store extracts that you clean in `code/data/` and sync authoritative copies to `i:/iplant/home/shared/esiil/Innovation_summit/Group_16/shared_data/pad-us/`.
- **NOAA Fisheries management regions** — Boundary shapefiles and metadata from the [NOAA Fisheries website](https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/). Document projection changes in your processing notebook.
- **Indigenous territories (Native Land Digital)** — Use the [API](https://native-land.ca/api-docs/) or download shapefiles. Confirm permissions before sharing beyond the team.

## Qualitative and policy sources
- **Co-management agreement texts** — Collect PDF/HTML versions from agency and Nation websites. Log citation details in `documentation/group-notes.md`.
- **Media narratives** — Export article metadata from LexisNexis or GDELT. Store CSV exports under `shared_data/media/` in the CyVerse folder.
- **Guardian program reports** — Reach out to community partners for approved summaries. Note usage constraints alongside the files.
